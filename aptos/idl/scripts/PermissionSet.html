FieldTypeDescription

granterHexStringAddress of oracle queue that is granting permissions to use its resources.
granteeHexStringAddress of account that is being assigned permissions to use a granters resources.
permissionu64Permissions being assigned to a resource. See SwitchboardPermission for enumerations and their descriptions.
enableboolFlag dictating whether permissions are being enabled or disabled.
