A web app enabling developers to create custom Switchboard feeds (Switchboard
Streams) or aSync functions, streamlining the process of integrating oracle
networks into their projects. DeFi platform's can create a custom feed for
tracking asset prices, ensuring accurate pricing information for swaps and other
transactions.
